Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026 · Version 1.2 · privacy@leafcigars.fyi

1 Who we are

LEAF ("LEAF," "we," "us") is a single-player cigar journal for iPhone. This policy covers the LEAF app and this website, leafcigars.fyi. The data controller responsible for your information is LEAF, reachable at privacy@leafcigars.fyi.

2 Data we collect

Your information falls into three kinds, each handled differently:

1 · Your private journal
The cigars you log and everything you add to them: your rating, your written review, tasting-note tags, your own read of how strong and how full the cigar felt, an optional photo, the vitola, the places you type, and price. This is shown only to you.
2 · Facts you add to the shared catalog
If you add a cigar the catalog is missing, its objective facts (such as brand, line, wrapper, origin, and size) become part of LEAF's shared catalog. Your opinions do not.
3 · Aggregate insights
De-identified, aggregate statistics drawn from ratings and logged cigars (see §6), which we may use ourselves and license or sell to cigar-industry partners.

Your account. Sign in with Apple is the only way to sign in to LEAF. Apple gives us an account identifier and, on your first sign-in, may give us your name and an email address, which may be an Apple private relay address that hides your real one. We do not send your name or email address to our analytics provider. To confirm you meet the age requirement (see §4), we collect your date of birth once. It is stored to enforce the age gate and is not shown to anyone.

Usage & device. With your consent (see §5), we collect pseudonymous product-analytics events describing which features you use, plus basic app and device info and an approximate location (such as country) derived from your IP address. These are tied to an app-generated identifier or your account identifier, never to your name or email. The events describe actions rather than journal content, with one narrow exception: when you save a log, the tasting-note terms you selected from LEAF's fixed list may be included. Notes you type yourself are filtered out and never sent.

3 How we use it

  • Run your account and store your journal.
  • Maintain the shared cigar catalog (facts only).
  • Show catalog information and similar cigars from the catalog (see §7).
  • Confirm you meet the 21+ eligibility requirement.
  • With your consent, understand which features are used, and diagnose errors, so we can improve LEAF.
  • With your consent, build the aggregate "Cigar Insights" described in §6.
  • Handle your requests, including data export, account deletion, and support.
  • Keep the service secure and comply with the law.

4 Age (21+) and your date of birth

LEAF is for adults 21 and older. This is a condition of using the service, not a tobacco sale. We collect your date of birth once, solely to confirm eligibility; it is enforced on our server and never displayed. We do not verify your identity, and LEAF does not sell tobacco or verify age on behalf of any retailer. LEAF is not directed to anyone under 21.

5 Analytics and your consent

We use PostHog (a US-based analytics provider) to understand product usage and to record app errors. It records actions, counts, and coarse error categories. It does not record your written review, notes you type yourself, the places you enter, price, your photos, your rating values (only whether you rated), your search text (only its length), your date of birth, your name or email address, or precise location. The identifier used is pseudonymous (an app or account identifier, not truly anonymous).

Where you are decides the starting point. In the EU, EEA, and UK, analytics stays off until you turn it on, and LEAF asks on first run. Everywhere else it is on by default and you can turn it off at any time. Either way, the choice you make is remembered and takes precedence over the regional default afterwards, including on a later launch, a new device, or a reinstall.

The control is Settings → Privacy → "Share Usage Data." While analytics is off, the analytics tool is not started and nothing is transmitted to it.

6 Aggregate "Cigar Insights"

We create de-identified, aggregate statistics from ratings and the cigars people log, for example a cigar's average rating, or how often a line is logged in a given city. This is aggregated to city level at most and does not identify you individually. We may use it ourselves and license or sell it to partners in the cigar industry for their market research. The "Share Cigar Insights" toggle in Settings controls whether your data is included.

We do not share your written reviews, your photos, your individual logs, your own strength and body read, or your precise location. Because it is de-identified and aggregated, this information is not your personal information. Before releasing any statistic we remove direct identifiers, exclude reviews and photos, limit geographic precision to city, and suppress results drawn from a small number of users, so that individuals cannot reasonably be singled out.

Like the analytics choice in §5, this toggle's starting position depends on your region: off in the EU/EEA/UK, on elsewhere. It is set once when your account is created, and your own choice always wins afterwards. The toggle is reversible anytime and is separate from the analytics choice in §5.

7 Recommendations and how they are made

LEAF can show similar cigars alongside a cigar's catalog page. Those comparisons are computed on your device from shared catalog facts (such as flavor descriptors, body, and wrapper) together with anything you have chosen to tell LEAF about your own taste. Your journal is not sent anywhere to produce them, and no other user's journal is used to produce yours. These are informational suggestions about cigar profiles, not advice, and not a prediction that you will enjoy a cigar.

8 Who we share with

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf, currently Supabase (database, authentication, and private photo storage; US), PostHog (analytics and error reporting; US), and Apple (Sign in with Apple). We may add or replace providers and will keep this policy current.
  • Cigar-industry partners: only the de-identified aggregate insights of §6, subject to the "Share Cigar Insights" toggle.
  • Legal & safety: when required by law, or to protect rights and safety.
  • A future owner, if LEAF is involved in a merger or acquisition.

We do not sell your personal, identifiable information, and we do not use it for targeted advertising. The only information we may sell is the de-identified, aggregate data described in §6, which is not personal information. The "Share Cigar Insights" toggle opts you out of it. Because we deal only in aggregate, de-identified information about our own users, and never individual records, we do not consider ourselves a "data broker" under state data-broker registration laws; if that changed, we would register as required.

9 Your rights & choices

  • Access / export your data: Settings → Account → "Export my data" produces a copy of your profile and every log you have made.
  • Correct your account information, or delete your account and everything in it: Settings → Account → "Delete account." This permanently erases your account, every log, rating, tasting note, and photo. Deletion runs as a server-side job so it finishes even if you close the app; it cannot be undone.
  • Analytics & insights: the two toggles in Settings → Privacy, reversible anytime.

US state privacy rights. If you live in a US state with a comprehensive consumer-privacy law, including California (CCPA/CPRA), Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Texas, Utah, Oregon, Montana, and others as they take effect, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and obtain a portable copy of your personal information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information and of targeted advertising and certain profiling. We do not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

Opt out of sale/sharing: use the "Share Cigar Insights" toggle in Settings, or email privacy@leafcigars.fyi. We also honor recognized browser/device universal opt-out preference signals, such as Global Privacy Control (GPC), as a request to opt out of sale/sharing where the law requires.

EU, EEA and UK rights. If you are in the EU, EEA, or UK you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability, and the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting processing already carried out. We rely on: performance of our agreement with you to run your account and store your journal; your consent for analytics (§5) and for Cigar Insights (§6), each withdrawable in Settings; our legitimate interests in keeping the service secure and working; and legal obligations, including the 21+ age requirement. Your information is processed in the United States by us and our providers, under the safeguards those providers offer. You may also complain to your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any right, email privacy@leafcigars.fyi; we may need to verify your identity.

10 Security & retention

Data is encrypted in transit. Every record is protected by row-level security scoped to your account, and your photos are stored in private, per-user storage that no other user can read. No system is perfectly secure.

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your active account records, your journal content, and your stored photos; copies may remain briefly in encrypted backups until they expire on our ordinary cycle, and we may keep limited security, fraud-prevention, or legal records where reasonably necessary. Facts you contributed to the shared catalog remain in the catalog, as do de-identified aggregate statistics, neither of which identifies you.

11 This website

leafcigars.fyi uses PostHog to count page views and basic interactions so we can see which pages are useful. It does not build a profile for anonymous visitors, we do not use it for advertising, and the site does not host accounts or journal data.

12 Children, changes & contact

LEAF is not intended for anyone under 21. LEAF is operated from the United States and your information is processed there. We may update this policy and will post the new effective date; material changes will be surfaced in the app. Questions or requests: privacy@leafcigars.fyi.
